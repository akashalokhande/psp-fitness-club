Gym Fitness

Gym workouts include strength training, weightlifting, and functional exercises to build muscle, improve endurance, and enhance overall fitness.

Yoga

A practice that combines physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to improve flexibility, balance, and mental well-being.

Zumba

A high-energy dance workout that blends Latin and international music with aerobic movements, making exercise fun and effective for burning calories.

Cardio

Exercises like running, cycling, and jumping rope that elevate heart rate, improve stamina, and enhance cardiovascular health.

Meditation

Meditation is a practice that involves focusing the mind to achieve mental clarity, emotional calmness, and self-awareness. It has been used for centuries in various spiritual traditions but is now widely recognized for its psychological and physical benefits.
